This Cookie Policy explains how StocksCo ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our Platform. By continuing to use StocksCo, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy, unless you choose to disable them through your browser or cookie preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites:

  • Function properly
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Provide analytics and insights

Cookies do not give us access to your device or personal files.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

a. Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the Platform to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Examples:

  • User authentication and login sessions
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Account access and navigation

b. Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with StocksCo so we can improve performance and usability.

Examples:

  • Page views and traffic sources
  • Feature usage and interaction patterns
  • Error tracking and load performance

Data collected is aggregated and anonymized where possible.

c. Functional Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and provide a more personalized experience.

Examples:

  • Language and region preferences
  • Dashboard layout settings
  • Notification preferences

d. Marketing & Communication Cookies

These cookies may be used to deliver relevant messages or promotions (where applicable).

Examples:

  • Referral tracking
  • Campaign performance measurement
  • Platform announcements

StocksCo does not sell user data to advertisers.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third-party services to place cookies on our Platform for:

  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Identity verification support
  • Payment processing functionality

These third parties are required to comply with applicable data protection laws.

4. How You Can Manage Cookies

You can control or delete cookies at any time through:

  • Your browser settings
  • Device privacy settings

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality, including login, trading, or transactions.

5. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, laws, or platform functionality. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies or data practices, contact us at: support@stocksco.io
