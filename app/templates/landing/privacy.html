At StocksCo, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Government-issued ID (for KYC verification)
  • Residential address

b. Financial Information

  • Wallet balances
  • Transaction history
  • Investment activity
  • Payment method details (processed securely via third-party providers)

c. Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Device type and browser
  • Log data and usage analytics
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify your identity (KYC/KYB compliance)
  • Process transactions and investments
  • Provide market data and portfolio insights
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Communicate important updates and notifications
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your information with:

  • Identity verification providers (KYC services)
  • Payment processors and financial partners
  • Analytics and infrastructure providers
  • Regulatory or legal authorities when required by law

All third parties are required to protect your data and use it only for authorized purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Data encryption
  • Secure servers and access controls
  • Regular security audits

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. Users are encouraged to protect their login credentials.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

StocksCo uses cookies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Improve performance and personalization

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Requests can be made via support@stocksco.io.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services
  • Meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

8. Third-Party Links

Our Platform may contain links to third-party websites. StocksCo is not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@stocksco.io
